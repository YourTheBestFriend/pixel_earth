2D Enemy AI by Sharp Coder

This project contains an example of an 2D player controller and a simple platformer AI.

- Open 'TestScene' from Scenes folder to launch an example scene
- Press Play: use A, D to move back/forth,  W to jump and W, S to move up/down the ladder 
- Observe the enemy AI movement when you get close to them
